Dilanne is trying to uncover the truth about her mother's murder as she travels to South Africa in search of answers. The British girl discovers sinister things about a hex placed on her family. When Evil Lurks - 10.06.2023

Pedro and his brother Jimmy stumble upon a pregnant malevolent entity in their small village. They attempt to vanquish it, unaware of the horrifying fate awaiting them. Embark on a wild and spine-chilling journey with the brothers as they confront the lurking evil."When Evil Lurks" is, in my opinion, one of the finest SHUDDER originals. Showcasing shocking and disturbing horror at its peak, this film demonstrates the perilous consequences of fucking with evil.
